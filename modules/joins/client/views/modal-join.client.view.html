Association for Academic Women Membership Form

Joining AAW is a great way to build your UF network and expand you knowledge and skills. To join, please complete the form below. After submitting, you will be redirected to an online payment screen for our $45 dues. Dues are used for events, member-only socials and an annual scholarship awarded to female UF doctoral students. The membership year spans from June 1st to May 31st; dues will reflect the year in which they are paid. If you would prefer to pay dues by check, please contact Marilyn Dumont-Driscoll at dumonmd@peds.ufl.edu…





If you would like to include a donation, please submit any additional amount with your membership dues. Thank you
