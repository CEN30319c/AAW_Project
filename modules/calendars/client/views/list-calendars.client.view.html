UF Webmail Calendar
1. Copy this link:
https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/aaw.florida%40gmail.com/public/basic.ics
2. Click the "UF WEBMAIL CALENDAR" button above and login with your Gatorlink
3. On the top of the page, click on "Add calendar" and from the drop down menu click "From internet"
4. Under "Link to the calendar" paste the link copied in step 1
5. Under "Calendar name" put any name you wish
6. Click Save and you're done!

3. Fill out the form that pops up (be sure that the event has a title, location, start date/time, end date/time, and a description)
