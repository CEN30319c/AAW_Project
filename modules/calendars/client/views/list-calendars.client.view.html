2. Click the "UF Webmail Calendar" button above and login with your Gatorlink
3. On the top of the page, click on "Add Calendar" and from the drop down menu click "From Internet"
4. Under "Link to Calendar" paste the link copied in step 1
5. Under "Calendar Name" put any name you wish
6. Click Save and you're done!

HOW TO ADD EVENTS

1. Click on the "Access this Calendar" button above and login
2. On the top of the page, click on "+ New"
3. Fill out the form that pops up (make sure that the event has a title, location, start date/time, end date/time, and a description)
4. Click Save and you're done!
